Tropical summer

In summer 2019, we spent a 2-week holiday in Vietnam, moving from South to North for wonderful family and leisure time.
We stopped at Sai Gon(Ho Chi Minh city), then headed to Ha Noi then Da Nang before coming back home to Finland.
We had 8 flights in total, as flying back and forth did not sound as its best, but we're alive and well.
It was unique, valuable memory we had had as a family, especially for my 1-year-old son whose name means "Love to explore"

During our trip, as lazy as we could, we mostly explored the local food and enjoy the weather at the beaches or the pools.
So if you share the same idea as us, then you are in the right place. Let's explore!
